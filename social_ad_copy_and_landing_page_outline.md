# Smoke Test Landing Page Outline

## General Structure

*   Headline: Problem-focused headline (e.g., "Need a Ride NOW?" )
*   Subheadline: Benefit-driven statement (e.g., "Get a reliable taxi in minutes.")
*   Simple Form: Collect basic contact information (Name, Phone, Brief Description of Need)
*   Clear Call to Action: (e.g., "Get Instant Quote", "Request Service Now")

## Specific Category Examples

### Taxis/Transportation

*   Headline: Stranded? Get a Ride NOW!
*   Form: Pickup Location, Destination, Number of Passengers

### Massage/Spa Services

*   Headline: Relax NOW! In-Villa Massage Available.
*   Form: Desired Service, Preferred Time, Location

### Home Repair/Maintenance

*   Headline: Urgent Repair? Get Help NOW!
*   Form: Type of Issue, Address, Availability
