## Taxi Ad Image Description

**Concept:** Split-screen ad showcasing the contrast between a stressful traditional taxi experience and the ease of using a modern taxi app.

**Left Side: The Problem (Stressful Tourist)**
*   Setting: A busy, chaotic tropical airport (Koh Samui preferred).
*   Character: A stressed tourist, looking overwhelmed.
*   Visuals: Sweaty, frustrated expression. Surrounded by luggage. Long taxi queue in the background. Unclear signage.
*   Color Palette: Muted, warm, slightly desaturated colors to convey discomfort and heat.

**Right Side: The Solution (Happy Tourist with App)**
*   Setting: Comfortable, modern taxi interior or a relaxed scene near a beach.
*   Character: A happy, relaxed tourist showing a phone screen.
*   Visuals: Smiling, confident expression. Holding a smartphone with a clean, modern taxi app UI. Taxi is clean and comfortable. Clear view of tropical scenery.
*   Color Palette: Vibrant, cool, saturated colors to convey ease and satisfaction.

**App UI Elements:**
*   Clean, modern design.
*   Easy-to-read map with GPS tracking.
*   Clear fare information.
*   Prominent "Book Taxi" button.
*   App icon should be visible and recognizable.

**Overall Style:**
*   Modern, clean, and visually appealing.
*   Vibrant colors to attract attention.
*   Clear contrast between the two sides to highlight the benefits of the taxi app.

**Target Audience:** Tourists visiting Koh Samui.
