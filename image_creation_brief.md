# Image Creation Brief

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the specifications for creating images for Kosmoi. The goal is to ensure consistency and quality across all visuals.

## 2. Target Audience

*   **Description:** Service Providers and Customers on Koh Samui seeking efficient solutions.
*   **Demographics:**
    *   **Service Providers:** Aged 25-55, Thai Nationals or long-term residents, Tech-Comfortable.
    *   **Customers:** Tourists (25-55 age range), Digital Nomads, and Local Residents.
*   **Interests:** Efficiency, Convenience, Local experiences, Fair pricing, Transparency.

## 3. Image Specifications

*   **File Format:** PNG, JPG, WebP
*   **Resolution:** 72 DPI for web
*   **Color Profile:** sRGB for web
*   **Dimensions:**
    *   **Standard Image:** 1200x628 pixels
    *   **Thumbnail:** 300x300 pixels
    *   **Banner:** 1920x1080 pixels
*   **Maximum File Size:** 500KB

## 4. Style Guide

*   **Overall Aesthetic:** Modern, Clean, Trustworthy, Efficient, Friendly.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary:** #007BFF (Kosmoi Blue)
    *   **Secondary:** #6C757D (Gray), #28A745 (Green - for success/availability)
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** Montserrat Bold
    *   **Body:** Open Sans Regular
*   **Imagery:** Combination of photographs (local scenes, people using services), illustrations (icons for features), and UI screenshots.
*   **Tone:** Informative, helpful, positive, and reassuring.

## 5. Subject Matter

*   **General Themes:** Ease of booking services, local Koh Samui lifestyle, benefits of using Kosmoi, showcasing providers, customer satisfaction.
*   **Specific Examples:**
    *   A tourist booking a massage on the beach.
    *   A local resident finding a repair service.
    *   Screenshots of the Kosmoi app/website UI showing a user booking a service.
    *   Smiling service providers.
    *   Infographics demonstrating the platform's efficiency.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

*   **Do:**
    *   Use high-quality, professionally looking images.
    *   Ensure images are relevant to the content and target audience.
    *   Use images that are consistent with the Kosmoi brand.
    *   Optimize images for web to ensure fast loading times.
*   **Don't:**
    *   Use blurry or pixelated images.
    *   Use copyrighted images without permission.
    *   Overuse stock photos that don't feel authentic.
    *   Use images that are misleading or deceptive.
