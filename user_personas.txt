## User Personas

**1. B2B - Sarah, the Marketing Manager**
*   **Demographics:** 35 years old, Marketing Manager at a SaaS company with 50-200 employees.
*   **Motivations:** Increase brand awareness, generate leads, improve marketing ROI, demonstrate the value of marketing to the executive team.
*   **Pain Points:** Difficulty tracking marketing campaign performance, lack of budget, difficulty demonstrating ROI, struggling to align marketing and sales.

**2. B2B - David, the CEO**
*   **Demographics:** 45 years old, CEO of a manufacturing company with 200-500 employees.
*   **Motivations:** Increase revenue, improve profitability, streamline operations, stay ahead of the competition.
*   **Pain Points:** Difficulty finding and retaining qualified employees, rising costs, supply chain disruptions, increasing regulatory burden.

**3. B2C - Emily, the Student**
*   **Demographics:** 20 years old, college student.
*   **Motivations:** Find affordable services, easily accessible, and reliable.
*   **Pain Points:** Limited budget, difficulty finding trustworthy service providers, time constraints due to studies.

**4. B2C - John, the Busy Professional**
*   **Demographics:** 38 years old, works full-time, married with young children.
*   **Motivations:** Save time and simplify tasks, find convenient and reliable services.
*   **Pain Points:** Lack of free time, difficulty balancing work and family responsibilities, feeling overwhelmed.

**5. B2C - Maria, the Retiree**
*   **Demographics:** 68 years old, retired.
*   **Motivations:** Maintain independence, find affordable services, stay connected with others.
*   **Pain Points:** Limited income, difficulty using technology, social isolation.
