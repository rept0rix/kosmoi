# Marketing Blitz Strategy

## Core Message:
Attract digital nomads and service professionals to the Kosmoi platform on Koh Samui by highlighting the ease and efficiency of accessing services, creating connections, and maximizing their time.

## Target Channels:
- Facebook Ads
- Google Ads
- TikTok Ads
- Influencer Marketing (Instagram, YouTube)

## Budget Allocation:
- Facebook Ads: 40%
- Google Ads: 30%
- TikTok Ads: 20%
- Influencer Marketing: 10%
